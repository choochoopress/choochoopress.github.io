CHOO CHOO PRESS

←

We're a Risograph zine press that publishes literature that interrogates and/or recreates the outside world. In order to create a truly immersive experience, we edit and create with design and tactile choices in mind. To this end, all of our books are printed in-house and are subsequently hand cut, collated, and bound. Print runs are typically 75-100 copies.

Each year, we publish 2 chapbook length books and a range of 8 fold zines. We prioritize historically marginalized voices, most typically queer and women artists.

You can shop our books online in our Etsy store. To see them in person, drop by these NY bookstores: McNally Jackson, Quimby's NYC, Black Spring Books, or Unnameable Books. We're also stocked in Boston at the Grolier. Just ask for the zine section! :)
