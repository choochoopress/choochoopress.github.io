Tay & Em

We're a small Risograph press with roots in Paris/NYC that publishes literary zines and poetry chapbooks with an emphasis on queerness, nostalgia, obsession, and states of liminality.
Shop our books online on our site or Fire Escap. To see them in person, drop by McNally Jackson or powerHouse Arena. Just ask for the zine section! :)
