Tay & Em

Taylor is a writer. Her favorite part of takeout is the fortune cookie. Emily is a designer. She still has her fifth grade crush’s Valentine’s day card.

We're a small Risograph press with roots in Paris/NYC that publishes literary zines and poetry chapbooks with an emphasis on queerness, nostalgia, obsession, and states of liminality. Contact us for submissions or just to say hi at choochoopress[at]gmail[dot]com. :)
