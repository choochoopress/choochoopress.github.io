CHOO CHOO PRESS

←

Choo Choo Press is a Risograph publishing project dedicated to poetry, artists' books, and thoughtful nonfiction.

We publish 3-4 books a year, mostly by queer and women artists. We strongly prioritize the physical reading experience. To this end, all of our books are printed in-house and are subsequently hand cut, collated, and bound. Print runs are typically 150-250 copies.

You can shop us online in our Etsy store. To see our books in person, drop by the following NY bookstores: McNally Jackson, Quimby's NYC, Black Spring Books, Topos Bookstore, Unnameable Books or the Grolier in Boston. Just ask for the zine section :)

Select poetry chapbooks are also housed at the Poets House library and at the Asian American Writer's Workshop, where they are available to read free of charge.
