CHOO CHOO PRESS

←

We're a Risograph zine press with a focus on ephemerality, printed matter, and bodily experience.

We publish 3-4 books a year, mostly by queer and women artists. All of our books are printed in-house and are subsequently hand cut, collated, and bound. Print runs are typically 75-100 copies.

You can shop us online in our Etsy store. To see them in person, drop by these NY bookstores: McNally Jackson, Quimby's NYC, Black Spring Books, Topos Bookstore, or Unnameable Books. We're also stocked in Boston at the Grolier. Just ask for the zine section! Select chapbooks are also available to read, free of charge, at the Poets House library.
